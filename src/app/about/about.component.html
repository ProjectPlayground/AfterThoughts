AfterThoughts- The Story:

The Beginning:

It wasn't a dark and stormy night. It was an early summer morning, and greatness awaited.

The Quest:

Our hero had set out months before, steely determination in her eyes. Destined for programming mastery, she joined the Epicodus Guild.

The Challenge:

Many trials stood between our hero and Devhood- this story is about the task set before her to earn the right to place AngularJS on her list of deeds.

"Create a site, for members of a guild, jousters or something like that."

Our hero, a lover of books, chose to to make a place where similar bibliophiles could gather remotely. Here they could receive suggestions for manuscripts, and discuss the texts.

And lo, AfterThoughts, crafted from metals and lightning, was created!
